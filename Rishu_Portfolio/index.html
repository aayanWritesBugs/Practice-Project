About

Rishabh Pant is a dynamic left-handed wicketkeeper-batsman known for his aggressive stroke play and match-winning performances. He represents India in all formats and plays for Delhi Capitals in the IPL.

Career Highlights

  • Debuted for India in 2017
  • Scored centuries in England and Australia
  • Key player in India's historic Gabba win (2021)
  • Captains Delhi Capitals in IPL
