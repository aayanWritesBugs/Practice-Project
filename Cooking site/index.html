Soya Rice Recipe

Ingredients -

  • Soya
  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Green chili
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Cumin seeds (jeera)
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Garam masala

Instructions

  1. Boil Soya Chunks:
    • In a pot, boil water with a pinch of salt.
    • Add the soya chunks and cook for 5–7 minutes until soft.
    • Drain and squeeze out excess water. Rinse once with cold water and squeeze again.
  2. Prepare Masala Base:
    • Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add onion and sauté until golden brown.
    • Add ginger-garlic paste and fry for 30 seconds until fragrant. Add tomato and green chili. Cook until the tomato softens.
  3. Add Spices:
    • Add turmeric, red chili, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
    • Add soya chunks and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes to coat them with the masala.
  4. Add Rice:
    • Add cooked rice to the pan. Mix gently so the rice doesn’t break.
    • Sprinkle garam masala and adjust salt.
    • Stir-fry for 2–3 minutes on low flame.
  5. Finish & Serve:
    • Turn off the flame. Add chopped coriander and lemon juice.
    • Mix gently and serve hot.
